# The Gemma 4 Good Hackathon

## Overview

The Gemma 4 Good Hackathon is a featured Google DeepMind competition on Kaggle that challenges participants to harness the power of Gemma 4 models to drive positive change and global impact.

**Dates:** April 2, 2026 – May 18, 2026 (Final Submission: May 18, 2026 at 11:59 PM UTC)

**Prize Pool:** $200,000

**Current Status:** 8,014 Entrants | 79 Participants | 79 Teams | 80 Submissions

---

## Your Mission

Create a solution that addresses a real-world challenge using Gemma 4 models. Your submission should demonstrate either:
- An application that helps millions
- A specialized model that could exponentially scale innovation

---

## Submission Requirements

Every valid submission **must** include:

### 1. Kaggle Writeup (Technical Report)
- **Purpose:** Proof of Work—verifies your video demo is backed by real engineering
- **Format:** Paper or blog-style report
- **Word Limit:** Maximum 1,500 words
- **Required Content:**
  - Title and subtitle
  - Architecture explanation
  - How you specifically used Gemma 4
  - Challenges overcome
  - Why your technical choices were correct
  - Track selection (mandatory)

### 2. Video Demo (Most Important)
- **Duration:** 3 minutes or less
- **Format:** YouTube link (must be viewable without login)
- **Goal:** Tell a compelling story—show the problem and how your Gemma 4 app solves it
- **Quality:** Dynamic, engaging, high-quality production

### 3. Public Code Repository
- **Source of Truth:** Code demonstrating Gemma 4 implementation
- **Requirement:** Publicly accessible (GitHub, Kaggle Notebook, etc.)
- **Note:** Private Kaggle Notebooks auto-publish after deadline
- **Must include:** Well-documented, clear Gemma 4 implementation

### 4. Live Demo
- **Format:** URL or files (publicly accessible, no login required)
- **Purpose:** Allow judges to experience your project firsthand
- **Attachment:** Include in Writeup under "Project Links" (URL) or "Files"

### 5. Media Gallery
- **Requirement:** Cover image (required to submit)
- **Content:** Images and/or videos associated with your submission

## Submission Process
